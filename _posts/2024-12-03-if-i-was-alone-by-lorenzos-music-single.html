"If I Was Alone" by Lorenzo's Music is a dark, slow-burning track with a heavy lo-fi bassline, marimba, and a chunky electronic beat. A mysterious, warbled guitar adds to the haunting atmosphere. The vocals both shouted and whispered, enhance the song's mixed mood.
